Day 0¶

On Tuesday September 19th we are in Room 310 Watson Building (R15 on the UoB campus map).

Morning¶

  • 10.00 - 10.25 - introduction to the course - talk slides [1].
  • 10.30 - 10.55 - Introduction to the Jupyter Notebook;

10 minute break.

  • 11.05 - 11.30 - getting our data Analyzing Brexit;
  • 11.35 - 12.00 - a problem of proportions.

2 hour break.

Afternoon¶

  • 14.00 - 14.25 - solving with simulation; Conditionals, functions and loops.
  • 14.25 - 14.55 - solving with simulation; Three girls in a family of four.

10 minute break.

  • 15.05 - 15.30 - improving our estimate. Assume the proportion of boys born in the UK is 0.513 [2]. How would we change our estimate?
  • 15.35 - 14.00 - back to Brexit. Formulating the null hypothesis. The population and the sample; parameter and statistic. Simulating samples, and therefore, statistics. The sampling distribution.
[1]Source files for building the slides, and source for all the pages in this website are always available in the course Github repository.
[2]Official UK government statistics give the birth ratio as 105.3. This the number of boys born for every 100 girls.
